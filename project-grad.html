Graduate Studies

BrandingPhotographyPrint DesignIdentity

A brand campaign for UNL Graduate Studies using warm photography and a sticker-based visual identity system to recruit prospective graduate students.

Client
UNL Graduate Studies
Category
Branding
Year
2025
Services
Branding, Photography, Print Design, Identity

The Brief

UNL Graduate Studies needed a campaign that felt human and aspirational — one that could speak to the many different journeys graduate students take. The solution was a sticker-forward identity: bold, collectible, and deeply Nebraska.

The sticker set spans everything from the Lincoln skyline to PhD loading bars, designed to live on laptops, water bottles, and welcome packets — turning every student into a brand ambassador.

Process

Every project follows a thoughtful process — from discovery through refinement to final delivery.

01

Discovery

Interviewed current graduate students. Identified 'belonging' and 'future possibility' as the two key emotional drivers to anchor the campaign.

02

Visual Strategy

Developed a sticker-based system as the hero vehicle — familiar, shareable, and tactile. Red, white, and cream palette anchored in Husker identity.

03

Illustration

Created 12+ sticker designs ranging from illustrated icons to typographic pieces like 'PhD Loading' and 'Grad 25'.

04

Photography

Shot lifestyle photography of students and campus environments to support the sticker system in print and digital contexts.

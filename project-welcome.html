Student Welcome

PhotographyEditorialArt Direction

An editorial photography project documenting UNL's student welcome events — capturing the energy, diversity, and excitement of new students beginning their college journey.

Client
UNL
Category
Photography
Year
2025
Services
Photography, Editorial, Art Direction

The Brief

UNL's student welcome events draw thousands of new students each fall, but the photography capturing these moments often defaulted to stiff, posed imagery. This project produced candid, editorial-quality photos that felt alive.

Images were used across digital and print channels including the UNL website, welcome packets, and social media campaigns throughout the academic year.

Process

Every project follows a thoughtful process — from discovery through refinement to final delivery.

01

Pre-Production

Scouted locations across campus ahead of the events. Created shot lists organized by time of day and lighting conditions.

02

Shoot

Captured 400+ images across two days, prioritizing candid moments and genuine emotion over posed group shots.

03

Curation

Edited down to 45 selects, then performed color grading and retouching in Lightroom and Photoshop.

04

Delivery

Provided a web-optimized library for digital use and high-resolution files for print, with usage rights documentation.

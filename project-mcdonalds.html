McDonald's Happy Meal

PackagingBrandingLogo DesignPhotography

A full packaging redesign of the McDonald's Happy Meal, introducing 'The Otto Meal' — a dog-themed limited edition concept that reimagines the iconic red box with a modern, personality-forward identity.

Client
Class Project
Category
Packaging
Year
2026
Services
Packaging, Branding, Logo Design, Photography

The Brief

This project challenged me to take one of the world's most recognizable fast food packages and make it feel fresh without losing its iconic DNA. 'The Otto Meal' is a limited-edition Happy Meal concept centered on Otto, a charming dachshund character who brings personality and warmth to every surface.

The identity spans the full packaging suite: the Happy Meal box, sandwich wrapper, drink cup, and sticker booklet — each surface carrying the blue-and-gold Otto palette while maintaining McDonald's core branding signals like the golden arches and the smile motif.

Process

Every project follows a thoughtful process — from discovery through refinement to final delivery.

01

Research

Audited existing Happy Meal packaging across markets. Identified key brand equities to preserve: golden arches, the smile motif, and the concept of surprise and delight.

02

Concept

Explored 12 character concepts before landing on Otto the dachshund — playful, universally loved, and ownable across a range of packaging shapes and surfaces.

03

Design

Built the full suite in Illustrator. Custom type lockups, the Otto illustration, and a modular grid system that scales from the box to the drink sleeve.

04

Production

Output print-ready dielines for box, wrapper, cup, and sticker sheet. Photographed finished physical prototypes on white seamless.

Charleston Games

BrandingLogo DesignPrint

A complete brand identity for Charleston Games, a competitive tabletop gaming tournament series targeting enthusiast players.

Client
Class Project
Category
Branding
Year
2025
Services
Branding, Logo Design, Print

The Brief

Charleston Games needed a brand that felt premium and competitive without alienating the approachable, community-driven spirit of tabletop gaming.

The resulting identity balances bold typographic architecture with warm, hand-crafted detail across logo, type system, color palette, and print collateral.

Process

Every project follows a thoughtful process — from discovery through refinement to final delivery.

01

Research

Analyzed competitor brands across gaming and entertainment events. Identified a gap for a more editorial aesthetic in the tabletop space.

02

Concept

Developed three distinct creative directions — Classic, Kinetic, and Editorial — selecting the Editorial path after class critique.

03

Design

Built the full identity system: wordmark, icon, type scale, color system, and pattern library.

04

Application

Applied the identity to tournament programs, player badges, and banner mockups.

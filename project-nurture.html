Nurture Nebraska

Logo DesignBrand IdentityArt Direction

Logo and complete brand identity for Nurture Nebraska, a nonprofit connecting older adults with younger generations through reflective storytelling and intergenerational mentorship.

Client
Nurture Nebraska
Category
Logo Design
Year
2025
Services
Logo Design, Brand Identity, Art Direction

The Brief

Nurture Nebraska needed an identity that communicated warmth, connection, and whimsy — something that could appeal to both older adults and the younger generations they mentor.

The brand centers on two illustrated characters holding hands, representing the intergenerational bond at the heart of the organization's mission. Extended across digital, print, and social assets.

Process

Every project follows a thoughtful process — from discovery through refinement to final delivery.

01

Brand Audit

Reviewed comparable nonprofits in the intergenerational space. Identified an opportunity for a warmer, more illustrated aesthetic.

02

Exploration

Sketched 30+ logo concepts. Explored wordmarks, emblems, and character-based marks before landing on the two-character holding-hands concept.

03

Refinement

Developed the final logomark in Illustrator, building a flexible system: full color, one-color, and reversed variants.

04

System Build

Extended the identity to typography, color palette, pattern system, and template suite for ongoing organizational use.
